Places To Eat

Bar Louie - The Promenade at Downey

Great place to gather for sporting events and to get drinks with friends.

The Nest - A Breakfast Joint

Hidden gem and local hole in the wall. Expect a wait but definitely worth trying. Favorite thing on the menu is the Chilaquiles and make sure to try the bacon waffle!

Acapulco - Restaurant & Cantina

Bottomless mimosas, enough said!

Tokyo Garden - Sushi Restaurant

Fresh fish, amazing nigiri and sushi rolls. Sake bombs and a cold Asahi.

Izakaya BIZAN - Japanese skewers + Sushi

Social space with amazing Japanese food, good vibes and drinks.
